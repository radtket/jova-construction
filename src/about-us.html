Who we are Business Profile

Today, JOVA Construction combines these two forces to provide an exceptional service and a unique product on the market.

Our Story Then and Now

JOVA Construction’s origin date back to 1986 when the company’s specialty was laying stone and ceramic in the residential sector. At first, it was a "one man show" featuring Mister Pépin, the father. In 1996, the enterprise became a family affair with the arrival of his son Jonathan, eager to learn the profession. Together they then steer the company towards the complete renovation of bathrooms. In 2001, Jonathan returned to school to learn more about all aspects of commercial construction. After working for 10 years in perfecting his offices, shops and condo towers construction, he completed all necessary certifications and became superintendent of construction.

Jonathan’s passion residing in custom home renovation, he returns to it with a strong commercial experience, combining the two with a full license of Contractor General.
